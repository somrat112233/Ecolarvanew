About EcoLarva

Our Mission

EcoLarva is dedicated to creating sustainable waste management solutions through innovative biotechnology. We specialize in using black soldier fly larvae to convert organic waste into valuable resources.

Our process not only reduces waste going to landfills but also produces high-quality protein for animal feed and organic fertilizer for agriculture.

With years of research and practical application, we've developed efficient systems that are scalable for both small communities and large urban areas.

Our Services

Our Services

Organic Waste Processing

We provide efficient systems for converting food waste and organic matter using black soldier fly larvae.

Protein Production

Our process yields high-quality protein that can be used as sustainable animal feed for poultry and aquaculture.

Organic Fertilizer

The byproduct of our process is nutrient-rich fertilizer that enhances soil health and plant growth.

System Installation

We design and install customized waste management systems for homes, businesses, and municipalities.
